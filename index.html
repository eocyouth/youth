who we are

EOC Youth

Emmanuel Outreach Centre Youth, a vibrant community where young people come together to grow in faith, build lasting friendships, and discover their God-given purpose. We believe that youth are not just the future of the church but an active, vital part of it today. Our mission is to inspire, equip, and guide the next generation of believers as they walk with Christ and serve others.
